List/Set/Dict Comprehensions: make list logic more compact

Examples:
[expr for var in list_name]
[expr for var in list_name if condition]
[expr for var1 in list_name1 for var2 in list_name2]
[expr for var in list_name if condition1 if condition2]

any more complex than these, better to spell it out in full (more readbale)
replace [] with {} for sets and dicts
